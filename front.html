    • FAQs

    Can I book train tickets at night using the website?

    Yes,absolutely! You can book tickets 24/7 using the website.

    Should I carry a printout of my e-Ticket while travelling

    Not at all. You can travel with a screenshot of the e-ticket. It is mandatory that you carry a valid government photo ID along with the screenshot of e-ticket.

    How early can I book train tickets?

    Train tickets can be booked three days prior to the date of your journery.This is applicable for tickets booked online as well as through the counters at stations.
